About us

AUVIC is an interdisciplinary club, made up of a group of passionate students at the University of Victoria. Our goal is to be a key competitor in the RoboSub AUV competition through drafting and building autonomous submarines. We also strive to teach students within the club valuable industry skills.

RoboSub
What is it?

RoboSub is a competition held by Robonation in San Diego California each year over a week at the end of July. Multiple teams from around the world come together to compete against and learn from each other. AUVIC has gone to RoboSub the past two summers in 2016 and 2017 with plans to go again in 2018.
